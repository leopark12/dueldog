OUR
MISSION

As a fully automated esports platform, our vision is for online gamers to compete against each other for cash and prizes. We hope to provide a unique new experience for gamers to compete in a secure and safe manner. With all the games that you guys love to play, we hope to be a platform that lets you guys not only have fun but have friendly competition and maybe even get some rewards!

GAMES AND SETTING UP

how to start playing

STEP 1:

To begin, register an account with us and then proceed to login through Steam.

STEP 2:

Select the game you would like to play and the entry fee you wish to compete for.

STEP 3:

Once matched with an opponent click the “Launch Game” button when available and start gaming.
